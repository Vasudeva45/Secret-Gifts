FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.

How can I place an order on your website?

To place an order, simply browse our collection, select the item you wish to purchase, and proceed to checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods for your convenience.

Can I include a personalized message with my gift?

Yes, during the checkout process, you can add a personalized message that will be included with your gift.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor the delivery status of your package.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer service team for assistance.
